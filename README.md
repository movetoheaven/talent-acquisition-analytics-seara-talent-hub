# Talent Acquisition Analytics for Seara Talent Hub
## Project Overview
Talent acquisition plays a critical role in ensuring sustainable business growth, especially for startups that rely on qualified talent to support rapid expansion. This project focuses on analyzing recruitment data for Seara Talent Hub, a fictional startup company, to gain insights into candidate demographics, skill availability, talent readiness, recruitment channel effectiveness, and candidate motivations.
Using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and interactive dashboard development, this project aims to support data-driven talent acquisition strategies and help build a stronger talent pipeline for future workforce needs.

## Business Problem
Seara Talent Hub aims to build a sustainable talent pipeline to support future workforce growth. However, limited visibility into candidate demographics, competencies, motivations, and recruitment channel effectiveness makes it challenging to identify qualified talent and optimize hiring strategies.

## Business Objective
To analyze candidate profiles, skill availability, talent readiness, and recruitment channel effectiveness in order to support data-driven talent acquisition decisions and strengthen Seara Talent Hub's future talent pipeline.

## Business Questions
What does the current talent pool look like?
What skills are most commonly available among candidates?
How ready are candidates to support startup workforce needs?
Which recruitment channels attract the most qualified candidates?
Are candidate motivations aligned with startup work environments?
