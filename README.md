# Talent Acquisition Analytics for Seara Talent Hub
## Project Overview
Talent acquisition plays a critical role in ensuring sustainable business growth, especially for startups that rely on qualified talent to support rapid expansion. This project focuses on analyzing recruitment data for Seara Talent Hub, a fictional startup company, to gain insights into candidate demographics, skill availability, talent readiness, recruitment channel effectiveness, and candidate motivations.
Using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and interactive dashboard development, this project aims to support data-driven talent acquisition strategies and help build a stronger talent pipeline for future workforce needs.

## Business Problem
Seara Talent Hub aims to build a sustainable talent pipeline to support future workforce growth. However, limited visibility into candidate demographics, competencies, motivations, and recruitment channel effectiveness makes it challenging to identify qualified talent and optimize hiring strategies.

## Business Objective
To analyze candidate profiles, skill availability, talent readiness, and recruitment channel effectiveness in order to support data-driven talent acquisition decisions and strengthen Seara Talent Hub's future talent pipeline.

## Business Questions
- What does the current talent pool look like?
- What skills are most commonly available among candidates?
- How ready are candidates to support startup workforce needs?
- Which recruitment channels attract the most qualified candidates?
- Are candidate motivations aligned with startup work environments?

## Project Workflow
1. Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
    - Handled missing values and inconsistent records.
    - Standardized data formats and categorical variables.
    - Prepared the dataset for analysis.
2. Data Transformation & Feature Engineering
    - Created a Skill Score metric based on the number of technical skills mastered by each candidate.
    - Developed a Competency Level classification:
      - Emerging Talent
      - Job-Ready Talent
      - High-Potential Talent
3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
    - Candidate demographics analysis
    - Skill availability analysis
    - Competency level analysis
    - Recruitment source analysis
    - Candidate motivation analysis
4. Dashboard Development
    - Designed interactive dashboards in Looker Studio.
    - Built visualizations to support talent acquisition decision-making.
## Key Insights
### Talent Pool Overview
    - 56.68% of candidates are students or fresh graduates, creating a strong pipeline of early-career talent.
    - The largest candidate pool originates from a small number of dominant provinces, highlighting potential focus areas for talent sourcing.
    - LinkedIn emerged as the most common recruitment channel among applicants.
### Skills & Competencies
    - SQL and Python are the most widely mastered technical skills among candidates.
    - Modern data visualization tools such as Looker Studio remain less commonly adopted.
    - 90.3% of candidates are classified as either Job-Ready Talent or High-Potential Talent, indicating strong workforce readiness.
### Recruitment Channel Effectiveness
    - LinkedIn generates both the highest candidate volume and the strongest talent quality.
    - Recruitment channel performance varies significantly when evaluated through competency levels.
### Candidate Motivation
    - Candidates are primarily motivated by:
      - Upskilling opportunities
      - Networking opportunities
    - These motivations align well with startup environments that emphasize continuous learning, adaptability, and career growth.

## Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis findings, the following recommendations can help strengthen Seara Talent Hub's talent acquisition strategy:
1. Prioritize LinkedIn as the Primary Recruitment Channel
LinkedIn consistently attracts both the highest volume of applicants and the strongest talent quality. Increasing recruitment efforts on this platform can improve sourcing efficiency and candidate quality.
2. Expand Early-Career Talent Programs
Since 56.68% of candidates are students or fresh graduates, internship, apprenticeship, and graduate development programs can be leveraged to build a sustainable talent pipeline.
3. Address Skill Gaps in Data Visualization
While SQL and Python are widely mastered, visualization tools such as Looker Studio show lower adoption rates. Providing targeted training programs can help enhance analytical capabilities across the talent pool.
4. Accelerate Hiring for Job-Ready Talent
With 90.3% of candidates classified as Job-Ready or High-Potential Talent, recruitment processes can be streamlined to reduce onboarding time and meet workforce demands more efficiently.
5. Strengthen Employer Branding Around Growth Opportunities
Candidates are primarily motivated by upskilling and networking opportunities. Highlighting learning, mentorship, and career development programs can improve candidate attraction and engagement.
